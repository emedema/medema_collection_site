English Papers

“Lost in time, and lost in space... and meaning”: Deviancy as a Structure for Meaning in Frankenstein and Adaptations

Above is an essay regarding Mary Shelley's Frankenstein, written for my ENGL 250: Introduction to Literary Theory class. I really enjoyed researching for and writing this essay. Learning about deviancy and its presence in Frankenstein and Frankenstein Adaptations, such as Rocky Horror Picture Show was a lot of fun.

Dead or Monstrous: A Feminist Analysis of Gender Roles in Lore’s “Elizabeth Bathory: Mirror Mirror”

This short analysis was also for ENGL 250. For these assignments we were supposed to analyze a piece of media of our choice. In this analysis I decided to perform a feminist analysis on the episode of Lore about Elizabeth Bathory.
